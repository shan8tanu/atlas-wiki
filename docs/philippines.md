## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Hepatitis A, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis. Rabies for extended stays.

**Health Insurance:** Travel health insurance recommended — private hospitals preferred.

**Advisory:** Dengue prevalent year-round. Carry insect repellent.
