## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis (rural areas).

**Health Insurance:** Comprehensive travel insurance essential — hospital deposits often required upfront.

**Advisory:** Air pollution in major cities. Carry prescription medications with documentation.
