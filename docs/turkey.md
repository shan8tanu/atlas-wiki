## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Hepatitis A, Typhoid recommended. Routine vaccinations sufficient for Istanbul.

**Health Insurance:** Travel health insurance strongly recommended.

**Advisory:** Good private hospitals in Istanbul and Ankara. Tap water not always safe outside major cities.
