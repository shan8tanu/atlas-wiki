## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Hepatitis A, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis. Malaria prophylaxis for rural areas.

**Health Insurance:** Travel health insurance required — minimum $10,000 USD coverage.

**Advisory:** Dengue risk in urban and rural areas. Carry mosquito repellent.
