## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Routine vaccinations sufficient. Japanese Encephalitis for extended rural stays.

**Health Insurance:** Travel health insurance strongly recommended — medical costs are high.

**Advisory:** World-class healthcare. Pharmacies may not stock Western-brand medications.
