## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Meningococcal meningitis (ACWY) mandatory for Hajj/Umrah pilgrims. Routine vaccinations recommended.

**Health Insurance:** Health insurance mandatory — included in e-Visa fee.

**Advisory:** Good healthcare in major cities. Extreme heat in summer months.
