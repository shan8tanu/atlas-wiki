## What is Atlas?

A community-maintained, citation-backed database of visa requirements exclusively for Indian passport holders. Every fee, processing time, and requirement maps to an official government or embassy source.

No affiliate links. No sponsored content. Just verified data.

---

## How It Works

**Browse** — Find your destination country on the interactive map or in the directory.

**Verify** — Every data point links to its official source. Check the embassy portal yourself.

**Contribute** — Spot outdated info? Every page has an "Edit this page" button that takes you directly to the data source. No coding required.
