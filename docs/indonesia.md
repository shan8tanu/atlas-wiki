## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis. Yellow Fever if arriving from endemic area.

**Health Insurance:** Travel health insurance required for medical evacuation coverage.

**Advisory:** Dengue and malaria risk varies by island. Bali has good medical facilities.
