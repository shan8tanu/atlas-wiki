## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Routine vaccinations sufficient. Yellow Fever certificate if arriving from endemic area.

**Health Insurance:** Travel health insurance recommended — Singapore has high medical costs.

**Advisory:** Excellent medical facilities. Dengue occurs but is well-controlled.
