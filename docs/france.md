## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Routine vaccinations (MMR, DPT, Polio). Hepatitis A for first-time travellers.

**Health Insurance:** Schengen travel insurance mandatory — minimum EUR 30,000 medical coverage.

**Advisory:** Excellent healthcare. Pharmacies (green cross) widely available.
