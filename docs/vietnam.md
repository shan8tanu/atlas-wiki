## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Hepatitis A, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis. Tetanus booster recommended.

**Health Insurance:** Comprehensive travel health insurance strongly recommended.

**Advisory:** Dengue and Zika virus risk in southern regions. Drink only bottled water.
