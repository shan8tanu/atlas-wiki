## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Hepatitis A & B, Typhoid, Japanese Encephalitis, Rabies (for rural travellers).

**Health Insurance:** Travel health insurance essential — medical facilities limited outside Phnom Penh.

**Advisory:** Malaria risk in rural and forested areas. Avoid untreated water.
