## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Routine vaccinations sufficient. No mandatory vaccinations.

**Health Insurance:** Travel health insurance essential — US medical costs are extremely high without insurance.

**Advisory:** World-class but expensive healthcare. Emergency room visits can cost thousands of dollars.
