## Public Health & Vaccinations

<div class="atlas-health" markdown>

**Recommended Vaccinations:** Routine vaccinations sufficient. Yellow Fever certificate if arriving from endemic area.

**Health Insurance:** Travel health insurance mandatory — no reciprocal healthcare for Indian citizens.

**Advisory:** Sun protection essential. Be aware of dangerous wildlife in rural/coastal areas.
